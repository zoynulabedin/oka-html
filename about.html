OUR CORE BELIEFS

We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons: God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit. These three are co-equal and co-eternal. We believe in one God who is the creator and sustainer of all things, who is holy, loving, just, and merciful.

We believe that Jesus Christ was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the virgin Mary, and is true God and true man. We believe in His sinless life, His substitutionary death on the cross, His bodily resurrection, and His ascension to the right hand of the Father.

We believe in the Holy Spirit who convicts, regenerates, sanctifies, and empowers believers for Christian life and service. The Holy Spirit indwells every believer and produces spiritual fruit in their lives.

We believe that the Bible is the inspired Word of God and is the final authority for faith and life. Scripture is inerrant in its original manuscripts and is sufficient for all matters of faith and practice.

We believe that man was created in the image of God, but because of sin, was alienated from God. All humanity is born with a sinful nature and is in need of redemption through Jesus Christ.

We believe that salvation is by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone. That alienation from God can be removed only by accepting God's gift of salvation which is made possible only through the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ.
